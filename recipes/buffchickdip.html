Buffalo Chicken Dip

Description

Buffalo Chicken Dip is an easy to make appetizer for parties. All you need is chicken, buffalo sauce, ranch, cream cheese, and cheddar.

Ingredients

  • Chicken breast
  • Frank's Red Hot Buffalo Sauce
  • Cream cheese
  • Cheddar cheese
  • Salt and pepper
  • Tortilla chips

Steps

  1. Season and Boil your chicken breast
  2. Pour in 1/2 cup of buffalo sauce
  3. Pour in 1/2 cup of ranch
  4. Put in a pack of cream cheese
  5. Eat up
