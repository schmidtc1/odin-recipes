Pollo Guisado

Description

This is my favorite meal of all time. Eat over white rice. It is delicious.

Ingredients

  • Chicken thighs
  • Recaito (from Goya)
  • Pidgeon peas
  • Chicken broth
  • Potatoes
  • Carrots if you like
  • Adobo seasoning
  • Sazon seasoning
  • Bay leaf

Steps

  1. With some oil in a large pot, season and sear the chicken thighs.
  2. Dice up some potatoes while the chicken sears.
  3. Put in the rest of the ingredients.
  4. Bring to a low boil and cover for a while.
